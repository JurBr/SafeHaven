Hello! I'm Jur Braakman. This website showcases my thesis research on the role of small wins in supporting circular innovation in the Rotterdam Makers District. Feel free to explore and contact me if you'd like to be involved!

About the Research

The project investigates how small wins within the Rotterdam Makers District may support broader circular transitions in the port of Rotterdam. It focuses on overcoming linear lock-ins and the pilot paradox by building on innovation capital and collaborative networks.

Key Concepts

  • Small Wins: Concrete, moderately scaled innovations with in-depth change potential.
  • Pilot Paradox: Niche innovations may succeed locally but fail to scale or spread due to structural barriers.
  • Innovation Capital: Framework capturing six capital forms—natural, industrial, human, social, cultural, and creative—used in port-city ecosystems (Jansen, 2020).

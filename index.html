Small Wins in a Safe Haven

Exploring Circular Innovation in the Port of Rotterdam

Hello! My name is Jur Braakman, and I am conducting master's research on the role of small wins in fostering a circular transition in the Rotterdam Makers District. This research contributes to the wider challenge of overcoming linear lock-in in Europe’s largest seaport.

Key Concepts

  • Small Wins: Concrete, in-depth and moderately scaled successes that help create momentum for systemic change.
  • Pilot Paradox: The phenomenon where innovations thrive in niche environments but fail to spread to the regime level.
  • Innovation Capital: A framework to assess six types of capital—natural, industrial, human, social, cultural, and creative—needed for transition.

Research Timeline

  • May–June 2025: Deep mapping and document analysis
